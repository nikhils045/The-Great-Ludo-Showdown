POINT SYSTEM

  • 🥇1st : 10 Points
  • 🥈2nd : 5 Points
  • 🥉3rd : 3 Points
  • 🐣4th : 0 Points
  • ❌Not Played : -1 Point

BONUS POINT

  • 💡All Tokens under 10 mins: +2 Points

ACCURACY CALCULATION

  • 🎯Winning as Rank 1: +1.0
  • 🥈Winning as Rank 2: +0.5
  • 🥉Accuracy as Rank 3: -0.1
  • 🐣Accuracy as Rank 4: -0.05
  • ✨Getting Bonus (+2): +0.1

PENALTY

  • ⚠️Not Played 3 Matches in a Row: Eliminated
  • ⚠️Playing as a Team: Disqualified
  • ⚠️Playing with a Mod Application: Disqualified

UNFORESEEN EVENTS

  • 🌐Network Issue: Should be Reported 5 mins After the Game Starts, or else it will be considered as Not Played
  • 📵Game Freeze: Tell us by tagging inside the Group chat or on the TL 5 mins After the Game Starts

Each Game will be monitored via SANDBOX

Why? It can help us to determine

  • 🎲Dice Roll
  • 🗣️No Badmouthing in Chat
  • 🏆To Check on Time Finish
  • 🚔To Catch Modders
